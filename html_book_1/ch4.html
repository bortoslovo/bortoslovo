Tapenade (Olive Spread)



This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always 
a big hit at parties. My father recommends:



"Make this the night before so that the 
flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room 
temperature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving 
it warm."



Ingredients



    

  • 1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
    • 

  • 2 large garlic cloves
    • 

  • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
    • 

  • 1 t. capers
    • 




Instructions



    

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend 
until as smooth as possi-ble.
    2. 


  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times 
until they are incorporated, but still retain some
texture.
    3. 


  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh 
basil garnish (optional).
    4.
