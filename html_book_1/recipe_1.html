Schnitzel

Serve with a salad of mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumber and onion.

"Easy Schnitzel for dummies"

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 cup dry breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons cheese
  • 2 teaspoons parsley
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Pound the chicken to 1/2 inch thickness.
  2. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  3. Place flour in a bowl.
  4. Combine mustard and egg.
  5. Combine breadcrumbs, cheese, parsley and garlic.
  6. Dredge chicken in flour.
  7. Dip in egg mixture.
  8. Dredge in breadcrumb mixture.
  9. Saut√© until browned in a skillet with oil.
